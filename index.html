About

I am a Research Fellow at Microsoft Research India, where I work with Akash Lal in the Programming Languages and Systems group on testing tools for concurrent systems such as Coyote.

I have been fortunate to work with amazing researchers in the past. I was advised by Sorav Bansal for my Master's Thesis on "Sound Translation Validation for LLVM" at IIT Delhi. I also did an internship at Columbia University with Junfeng Yang working on program analysis for Upgradvisor.

I graduated with a B.Tech and M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Delhi in 2022. For more details, check my CV or send me an email.
